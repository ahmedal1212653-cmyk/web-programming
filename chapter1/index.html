🌮 Delicious Beef Tacos

These delicious beef tacos are quick and easy to make, packed with flavor, and perfect for any meal.

Ingredients

  • 500g ground beef
  • 1 packet taco seasoning
  • 8 taco shells
  • 1 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup shredded cheese
  • 1/2 cup sour cream

Method

  1. Cook the ground beef in a pan over medium heat until browned.
  2. Add taco seasoning and follow packet instructions.
  3. Warm the taco shells in the oven.
  4. Fill each shell with the cooked beef.
  5. Add lettuce, tomatoes, cheese, and sour cream.
  6. Serve immediately and enjoy!
